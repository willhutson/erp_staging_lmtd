# Hub Overview

The Hub is your central command center in SpokeStack. It provides quick access to all platform features and shows important information at a glance.

---

## Hub Layout

### Navigation Cards

The Hub displays module bundles as interactive cards:

#### ERP Bundle (Indigo)
Core business operations
- Briefs, Time Tracking, Leave, Team, RFP

#### Agency Bundle (Emerald)
Professional services management
- Clients, Retainers, Projects, Resources, CRM

#### Marketing Bundle (Purple)
Digital marketing tools
- Listening, Trackers, Media Buying, Analytics, Builder

#### Client Portal (Cyan)
White-label client access
- Dashboard, Approvals, Deliverables, Reports

---

## Quick Actions

From the Hub, you can:

1. **Navigate to any module** - Click a bundle card to expand, then select a module
2. **View recent activity** - See what's changed across the platform
3. **Access announcements** - Read team-wide messages
4. **Check pending tasks** - See items requiring your attention

---

## Widgets

The Hub can display various widgets based on your role:

### For All Users
- **My Tasks** - Briefs assigned to you
- **Timesheet Summary** - Hours logged this week
- **Leave Balance** - Available PTO days
- **Announcements** - Team messages

### For Team Leads
- **Team Workload** - Capacity overview
- **Pending Approvals** - Items needing your approval
- **Deadline Alerts** - Upcoming due dates

### For Leadership/Admin
- **Pipeline Value** - RFP potential revenue
- **Utilization Rate** - Team efficiency metrics
- **Budget Overview** - Project financials

---

## Customization

You can customize your Hub experience:

1. **Rearrange Widgets** - Drag and drop to reorder
2. **Hide/Show Widgets** - Toggle visibility in settings
3. **Theme** - Light or dark mode in preferences

---

## SpokeChat Quick Access

The megaphone icon in the header provides quick access to SpokeChat:
- View unread messages (badge shows count)
- Jump to recent conversations
- Send quick messages
